about

Asmita is IIIT Allahabad’s annual sports festival. After multiple successful editions in the past, it has become a flagship sports festival in North India. The word Asmita signifies glory and the feeling of oneness, and the festival rightly lives up to its name by bringing together the best players and teams of multiple renowned institutes on the sports field.
